About the Company

Standard Chartered is a British multinational bank with operations in consumer, corporate and institutional banking, and treasury services. Despite being headquartered in the United Kingdom , it does not conduct retail banking in the UK, and

around 90% of its profits come from Asia, Africa, and the Middle East.
